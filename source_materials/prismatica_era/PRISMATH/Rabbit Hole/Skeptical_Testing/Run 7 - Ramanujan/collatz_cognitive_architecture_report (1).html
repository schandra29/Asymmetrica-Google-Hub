Key Insights

  • Thought chains reinforce a search → reconcile → consolidate motif, with Balanced-asymmetric acting as a flexible staging bridge.
  • Equilibrium occupancy aligns with strong time in Support and Exploration, while Balanced remains transient but strategically central.
  • Survival and hazard reveal attention-span differences: regimes with rising hazard benefit from timely hand-offs; plateaus signal stable focus windows.
